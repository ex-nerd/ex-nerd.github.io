Type 1 Diabetes Resources

July 7, 2017

I plan to eventually write about a bunch of these topics individually. Until then, here is my collection of links to a number of diabetes-releated topics that I have found especially helpful.

Blogs to read

  • Melitta is my favorite advocate about adult-onset T1, and always has great information with a strong emphasis on science and research.
    • http://adultt1diabetes.blogspot.com/
  • Gary Scheiner is the author of the amazing Think Like a Pancreas. He also runs a diabetes consulting service and writes a lot of great stuff about dealing with T1.
    • http://integrateddiabetes.com/
  • Dr. Ponder’s Sugar Surfing method of dynamically managing blood sugar levels should be a tool available to everyone who uses insulin. His blog has great info in addition to the book.
    • https://www.sugarsurfing.com/
  • Digital magazine dealing with all kinds of things for both T1 and T2.
    • https://diatribe.org/
  • News about science and technology related to T1.
    • http://insulinnation.com/
  • Has a lot of information about all kinds of diabetes, but focuses mostly on topics relevant to people with T1.
    • https://asweetlife.org/
  • Magazine/blog that focuses on both T1 and T2, but still occasionally has some useful information.
    • https://www.diabetesselfmanagement.com/
  • Joslin is one of the most respected research institutions for T1.
    • http://blog.joslin.org/
